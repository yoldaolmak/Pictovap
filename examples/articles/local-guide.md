# Hidden Gems of the Pacific Northwest

Beyond the bustling streets of Seattle and Portland lies a rugged, untouched wilderness.

## The Olympic Peninsula

A vast area featuring temperate rainforests, alpine peaks, and wild coastal beaches. The Hoh Rain Forest is a must-visit, with moss-draped trees creating a magical atmosphere.

## Columbia River Gorge

Stretching for over 80 miles, the gorge offers spectacular waterfalls, including the famous Multnomah Falls. Hiking trails range from easy paved paths to grueling climbs.

## Practical Information

* **Best Time to Visit:** Late spring to early fall.
* **What to Pack:** Rain gear is essential year-round.
* **Getting Around:** Renting a car is highly recommended as public transit is limited outside major cities.
