# The Best Coffee Shops in Capitol Hill, Seattle

Capitol Hill is the beating heart of Seattle's coffee culture. Whether you need a quiet place to work, a lively spot to meet friends, or just an exceptional pour-over, this neighborhood has it all.

## Espresso Vivace

A true Seattle institution, Espresso Vivace is famous for bringing Northern Italian roasting styles to the Pacific Northwest. 

### The Experience

The Brix location offers a bustling, European-style atmosphere. Their latte art is legendary, and the Cafe Nico—an espresso with steamed half-and-half, orange zest, vanilla, and cinnamon—is a must-try.

## Analog Coffee

Tucked away on Summit Avenue, Analog Coffee feels like a neighborhood secret. It has a distinctly analog vibe, complete with a fantastic vinyl collection playing in the background.

### The Coffee

They proudly serve Herkimer Coffee. It's the perfect spot for purists who want an expertly pulled shot of espresso or a meticulously crafted cold brew.

## Starbucks Reserve Roastery

While Starbucks is ubiquitous, the Reserve Roastery on Pike Street is a completely different experience. It's essentially a theme park for coffee lovers.

### The Space

The massive, copper-clad roasting casks are mesmerizing. You can watch the entire roasting process while enjoying exclusive, small-batch coffees and freshly baked Italian pastries from Princi.
